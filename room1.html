📸 Air Pollution

Industrial Areas and Environmental Impact

Air pollution represents one of the greatest threats to our planet. Gas and particle emissions from industry, transportation, and burning of fossil fuels affect the quality of the air we breathe.

Effects:

  • Impact on human health (respiratory, cardiovascular diseases)
  • Climate change
  • Destruction of natural ecosystems
  • Acid rain that damages forests and lakes
