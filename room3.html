üå± Tutorial: How to Make a Greenhouse from a PET Bottle

Step by Step: Mini PET Greenhouse

A plastic bottle can have a new life! Here's how you can create a mini greenhouse for your plants:

1

Cut a PET bottle in half (with the help of an adult)

2

Keep the bottom part as a container for soil

3

Use the top part as a transparent cover (greenhouse)

4

Plant seeds and cover with the top part

5

Water regularly and watch your plants grow!
